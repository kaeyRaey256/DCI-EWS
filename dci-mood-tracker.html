How It Works
The Mood Tracker book uses colour-coded squares — one per day. Students colour each day's square with the emotion they felt: Green for Happy, Yellow for Neutral, Red for Distressed, and more. When scanned, our AI extracts these patterns, builds timelines, and flags students who may need support before crisis hits.
